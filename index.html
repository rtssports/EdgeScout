📌 Pinnacle Sharp
Uses Pinnacle's de-vigged line. Most reliable — Pinnacle is the sharpest book and sets the market.
⚡ Power
p_true = p_implied^(1/k), solve Σp=1
Corrects favorite-longshot bias. Most accurate for props and multi-outcome markets.
✖ Multiplicative
Scales each implied probability by the total overround. Standard industry method.
➕ Additive
Subtracts vig equally across outcomes. Simple and fast, best for balanced 2-way markets.
