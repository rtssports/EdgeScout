📖 De-vig Methods Explained

Four methods for removing bookmaker vig to find true probabilities:

📌 Pinnacle Sharp
Uses Pinnacle's de-vigged line directly. Most reliable — Pinnacle is the world's sharpest book and sets the market. Best when Pinnacle has the market.
➕ Additive
Subtracts vig equally from each outcome's implied probability. Simple, fast. Works well for balanced 2-way markets. Underestimates longshot true probability.
✖ Multiplicative
Scales each implied probability by the total overround. More accurate than additive for asymmetric markets. Standard industry approach.
⚡ Power
p_true = p_implied ^ (1/k), solve Σp=1
Iteratively solves for exponent k that normalizes probabilities. Corrects favorite-longshot bias — books pad longshot prices, Power accounts for this. Most accurate for props and multi-outcome markets.
