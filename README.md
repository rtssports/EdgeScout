# EdgeScout

AI-powered sports betting edge finder — true probability engine, +EV detection, arbitrage scanner, Kelly sizing, and bet tracker across all major markets and sportsbooks.


EdgeScout Pro is a browser-based sports betting analytics tool that calculates true fair probabilities for every outcome across moneyline, spread, total, and prop markets using four de-vig methodologies simultaneously — Pinnacle Sharp, Additive, Multiplicative, and Power — so you can identify where sportsbooks are mispricing lines and deploy your liquidity with a mathematical edge.

Features

🧮 Probability Engine — side-by-side true probability calculation using all four de-vig methods

📈 Positive EV Finder — surfaces bets where soft book odds exceed the fair line across any market

⚡ Arbitrage Scanner — detects guaranteed-profit opportunities across books in real time

📊 Odds Screen — best available odds across all books for every market and outcome

⚗️ Kelly Criterion Calculator — optimal stake sizing with full, half, quarter, and tenth Kelly fractions

📋 Bet Tracker — log, grade, and analyze your full betting history with P&L and ROI tracking

⬇ CSV Export — export probabilities, EV bets, arbitrage, and bet history for external analysis

Powered by The Odds API. Bring your own API key — free tier available at the-odds-api.com.
